Day 1: Amboseli National Park

You will be picked up from our hotel or airport and drive to Amboseli National Park, game drive en route. Arrive in time for lunch at the AA Amboseli Lodge. After lunch, you will take part in your first afternoon game drive with the opportunity to see lion, elephant, leopard, cheetah, gnu, giraffe, and the highest mountain in Africa, Mount Kilimanjaro. Later in the evening, you will return to the lodge for dinner and your overnight stay.

Day 2: Amboseli - Tsavo West National Park

After an early breakfast, you will take off on an exciting game drive on your way to Voyager Ziwani Camp, arriving in time for lunch. After lunch, you will take off on a nature walk to experience the park animals and the opportunity to see nocturnal wildlife at close range. The camp is close to the farm and has a chance to see how local farmers cultivate their farms. After your tour, you will return for dinner and your overnight stay at the Voyager Ziwani Camp.

Day 3: Tsavo West - Taita Wildlife Sanctuary

After breakfast, you will drive to Taita Wildlife Sanctuary; enjoying an exciting game drive along the way. You will arrive in time for lunch at the Taita Wildlife Lodge and then take off for an afternoon game drive with the opportunity to see lion, elephant, leopard, cheetah, and more! Later in the evening, you will return for dinner and your overnight stay at Sarova Saltlick Lodge built on stilts providing guests with the chance to see wildlife at close range.

Day 4: Tsavo East National Park

You will rise early and take off for a sunrise game drive with breakfast in hand. You will make your way to the gate driving to Tsavo East. Tsavo is the largest park in Kenya and is the place where you will have the best chance to see the "Big Five". You will arrive in time for lunch at Ashnil Aruba Lodge. After lunch, you will take off on an afternoon game drive with the opportunity to see more lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, giraffes, and more before heading to the lodge for dinner and your overnight stay.

Day 5: Tsavo - Mombasa

You will rise early and take part in a sunrise game drive before returning to the lodge for breakfast. After breakfast, you will check out and drive to Diani Beach, which is well known for its clear sandy beaches. End of the itinerary.
