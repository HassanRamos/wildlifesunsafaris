Wild Life Sun safaris

Wildlife sun safaris is a tour company registered in Kenya under the ministry of tourism and the laws of Kenya.

With over 20 years experience, we specialize in the organization of tours and safaris in this fascinating, exotic land .

All the aircrafts and vehicles we use are insured according to the IATA (International standard). Our clients have medical cover both on the ground and air. A flying doctor service is available 24 hours for those who are on safari within East Africa.

With our vast experience of tracking animals and knowing where they are, you will be forgiven for imagining that the animals are tracking you!
