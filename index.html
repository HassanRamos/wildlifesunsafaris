Kenya is the premier safari country. It is a land of contrasts, comprising of arid deserts, rich savannahs, snow-capped Mount Kenya, the Great Rift Valley, lakes and palm-fringed sandy beaches. The country boasts the widest array of game species in Africa, making it the ultimate travel destination. The hospitality of the people is known the world over and you will be met with beaming smiles and Karibu Kenya!

There are no mileage limitations on our game drives on all our road safaris. We offer the flexibility to meet our customers interests, budget and specific tastes.

Beach Tours

The Kenyan coast boasts miles and miles of unspoiled palm fringed beaches along the warm inviting waters of the Indian Ocean.Here the wilderness meets the sea and the ocean itself holds a world of spectacular coral reefs teeming with life and color..

Cultural Tours

Kenya’s indigenous population represents more diversity than any other African country. Coastal towns such as Malindi and Lamu thrive on a curious blend of ancient Arab and African culture. Try us, you will not be dissappointed..

Sporting Safaris

Kenya is a reputable sporting nation and many of its sportsmen and women are world champions. While football is the most popular sporting event in the country, athletics is the leading international sport. Other sports in Kenya include boxing, cricket, motoring (Safari Rally), archery, netball and basketball..
